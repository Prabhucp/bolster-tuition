Sainik School Entrance Coaching

Bolster Tuition provides focused coaching for students preparing for the All India Sainik Schools Entrance Examination — AISSEE, conducted for admission into Sainik Schools across India.

Sainik Schools focus on academics, discipline, physical fitness, leadership training, and character development. They also help students build a strong foundation for future entry into the National Defence Academy — NDA and other defence services.

AISSEE Exam Eligibility & Syllabus

Class 6

Eligibility
  • Age should generally be between 10 and 12 years.
  • Age is calculated as of March 31 of the admission year.
  • Student should be studying in or have passed Class 5.
  • Boys and girls are eligible, subject to official rules.
Syllabus
  • Mathematics
  • Intelligence / Reasoning
  • Language
  • General Knowledge

Class 9

Eligibility
  • Age should generally be between 13 and 15 years.
  • Age is calculated as of March 31 of the admission year.
  • Student should be studying in or have passed Class 8.
  • Boys and girls are eligible, subject to official rules.
Syllabus
  • Mathematics
  • Intelligence / Reasoning
  • English
  • General Science
  • Social Science

Qualification Criteria

To qualify in AISSEE, students must meet the required minimum standards:

  • Minimum pass mark: 25% in each subject / section.
  • Aggregate mark: 40% overall.
  • Falling short in even one section can affect the overall result.
  • Balanced preparation is necessary for better performance.

Why the Exam is Challenging

AISSEE is a competitive entrance examination where thousands of students apply every year for a limited number of seats. Students need strong basics, regular practice, speed, accuracy, confidence, and disciplined preparation.

Bolster Coaching Method

At Bolster Tuition, we follow a focused and realistic approach for Sainik School entrance preparation.

  • We first conduct a short 20-minute screening test to understand the student’s current level.
  • Students who secure 60% or above in the screening test will be considered ready for our focused Sainik coaching program.
  • We recommend a minimum of one year of preparation for serious AISSEE coaching.
  • Our main coaching focus will be on Mathematics, Intelligence / Reasoning, and English.
  • For General Knowledge, General Science, and Social Science, guidance and learning assistance will be provided.

Guidance Collaboration

Bolster Tuition also collaborates with Retd. Lt. Col. Jayavel, who guides students and parents about Sainik School preparation and defence-related guidance.

About Bolster Tuition

Strong Foundations. Personal Guidance. Honest Teaching.

Bolster Tuition Centre was started with a simple but strong purpose — to help students understand their subjects clearly, build strong basics, and learn with confidence in a disciplined and supportive environment.

Our Beginning

Bolster Tuition Centre was founded by Kalpana Selvaraj on February 15 2021 with just 3 students, a passionate educator who has always believed in guiding students with patience, structure and personal care. Now, the centre has grown into a small but strong academic community with a team of dedicated teachers an a growing number of students.

In April 2022, Prabhu Chinnia Palainappan joined her and began coaching a student for the Sainik School Entrance Examination. What started as one focused class gradually expanded into Mathematics guidance and English Grammar classes later into a stronger academic framework for the centre.

What We Believe

Every child learns differently. Some students understand quickly, while others need more time, repeated explanation and patient guidance. At Bolster Tuition, we respect this difference and teach according to each student’s learning level.

Teaching Philosophy

We believe discipline and enjoyment must go together. Our classes are structured and respectful, but also friendly and encouraging.

Strong Basics First

If a student struggles with a concept, we do not rush forward. We go back to the foundation and rebuild understanding step by step.

Commitment to Parents

We value the trust parents place in us. We maintain honesty in feedback, teaching and expectations so that real academic growth can happen.

Our Future Vision

Our aim is to grow responsibly by strengthening online classes, expanding academic programs and maintaining the same quality and personal attention.
